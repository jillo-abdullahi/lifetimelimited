Job Description Writing

Jobs are usually described in terms of the functions or tasks they fill without reference to the cultural or communications environment of the organization. We at Lifetime, helps the company’s focus on the productive aspects of individuals, that is the ability to adapt to changing circumstances and the individual ability to innovate and find solutions to problems. This helps the organization fit individuals into clearly defined roles reduces capabilities for performance and inhibits innovation. We try to make sure the evaluation criteria of the position will promote the type of activities to enhance the success of the business.

Environmental Consultancy

Environmental Impact Assessments

The environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) task is for compliance with Kenya’s legislation which requires that an EIA be conducted on developments. This is listed under the Environmental Management and Co-ordination Act 1999. Our team of experts and associates carries out EIA to determine the potential impact on environment, society and natural resources and helps to identify and analyze alternatives to proposed developments.Environmental Impact Assessment Reports in accordance to EMCA 1999 Laws of Kenya
Specifics inter-alia:-

  1. Development of terms of reference (TOR) for any project.
  2. Screening for environmental impacts
  3. Development of environmental management plans (EMP)
  4. Development of mitigation factors
  5. Writing and editing environmental reports
  6. Peer review of reports
  7. Interpretation of approval conditions
  8. Monitoring and evaluation of environmental impacts
  9. Coordinating approvals by NEMA
  10. Attending site meetings during the projects implementation
  11. Carrying out environmental and occupational health and safety exercises during project implementation.

Environmental Auditing

The key objectives of an environmental audit therefore are to: Determine how well the environmental management systems and equipment are performing. Verify compliance with the relevant national, local or other laws and regulations. Minimize human exposure to risks from environmental, health and safety problems. As the prime objective of audits is to test the adequacy of existing management systems, our team of experts fulfills the fundamental roles of monitoring and evaluation of environmental performance and analysis of all health and safety concerns and thereafter making recommendations on corrective and remedial measures to be implemented under the environmental management plan. Employee Satisfaction Survey

We format the survey by using “anonymous forced response.” This process allows the respondents to mark their response in terms of agreement or disagreement with the statements. The method helps in maintaining anonymity and gives quick response. The promise of anonymity helps some employees overcome the fear of negative job implications for answering questions honestly. As a result, most employees feel that they can be more candid in this type of survey.
