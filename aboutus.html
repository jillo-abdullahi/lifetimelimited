Our Uniqueness

Lifetime Limited provides services to both private, and public sectors through its deep knowledge of issues relevant to specific industries. Our industry experts have market, strategic, functional and technology expertise. We serve the county governments, public authorities, and education and health institutions, FMCGs, financial, telecommunication, energy, manufacturing and service sectors. In addition there is a specialist middle market group to meet the needs of dynamic and growing businesses.

Our Focus

Lifetime Limited works with clients on environmental, research and HR issues that cut across their strategic intent, organizational design, market research and surveys (Customer satisfaction Survey, Employee satisfaction survey, Work Environment and Employee motivation, Execution Audit), information system management and project management through the application of functional and industry expertise.

Our People

From graduate entry to experienced industry experts, our people are characterized by a willingness to work as part of a team and by their industry and functional expertise.

How We Work With Clients

We work collaboratively with our clients basing our work on an enterprise's values to develop solutions in the best interests of our clients and their people. We ensure:

  • Objectivity in our recommendations
  • Relevance through in-depth knowledge of key business functions; and
  • Our teams' skills match the specific client issues drawing on the wide expertise of our people.
