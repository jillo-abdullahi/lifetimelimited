Occupational Health and Safety

An Occupier of a workplace and/or Employer, has an obligation  under the Occupational Safety and Health Act (OSHA), 2007, laws of Kenya, to carry out appropriate risk assessments in relation to the safety and health of persons employed and, on the basis of these results, adopt preventive and protective measures to ensure that under all conditions of their intended use, all chemicals, machinery, equipment, tools and process under the control of the occupier are safe and without risk to health to employees.
